This website is founded by a very dedicated group of memebr from all around the world. We aspire to teach and inspire the minds of young photographers. Most of us enjoy looking at the photos of others but when it comes to grabing the camera and thinking of all the ways we could take photos, we just find it as too much work.

With Life In photos we want the public to look at the photos taken by us and feeling inspired to go out and capture the beautiful moments in everyday life. There are so many young people who don't feel like they're worthy enough to take photos, but the truth of the matter is that they are.
